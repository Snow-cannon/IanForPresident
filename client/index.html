I am a senior this year and very excited to move through our last steps of Hudson High with everyone. I transferred into Hudson High as a freshman and previously went to school in Framingham (they don't have 8th grade in the highschool). From there I moved through each year with more and more appreciation for what held the school up and what could be changed to make it the best it can be. My qualifications for leadership of the class include leadership training through the BSA, coaching foil fencing for a year, and training new employees at my workplace for the last two years.

The main changes I want to accomplish include making fundraisers a more common and fun occurrence, setting up a useful system for the Hallway TVs, and getting more interesting use out of the class boards. I will also have a google form set up at strategic places throughout the school so that any senior can submit requests directly to the student government.
