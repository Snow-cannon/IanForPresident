Student Media




There are many ways that students get information throughout the school year. Some are well made and consistently updated such as the HHS round robin and morning announcements. Others could be worked on to improve the way students can get to know whats going on.

Friday Morning Lights

  • Friday morning lights should show each and every sports team's successes, not just wins, and display more than just the popular sports (this will help get more people back into the sports programs that need people).

HTVSHall

  • The HTVSHall TV system is, in my opinion, poorly used. If we need to put a map of the school on the wall it doesn't need a monitor. However, I do think the system can be very useful and should still be run. The Friday morning lights are great and an incredible asset to the school community, but it should not be used on repeat throughout the school for the following week (especially with no audio). I suggest we make an enrichment that helps create the HTVS slides which could be set on repeat throughout a day. Things to include are events, sports updates, the schedule, and general announcements (which can be submitted by teachers for clubs). The more we can put in the HTVS the better, and the less poster clutter will be around the school.

Posters

  • Posters are useful, but we should make sure to consolidate the old posters and take them down ASAP. If the posters in the halls are not up to date people will stop reading them and they will serve a limited purpose.

Class Boards

  • Class boards are useless right now and the only purpose they server is to show how empty the school feels its activity is. They should show event pictures, autograph boards for events, and if any inter-grade comps are won they should get a banner or certificate for the board. If nothing else the boards should show what each of the classes have been up to.
